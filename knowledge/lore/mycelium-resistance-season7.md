# The Mycelium Resistance vs HEP (Season 7 Turf War)

## Overview

The Season 7 Turf War was the defining server-wide storyline of Hermitcraft Season 7 — a conflict between two factions over whether the Shopping District's ground should be covered in **grass or mycelium**. What sounds absurdly trivial became one of the most entertaining and elaborate storylines the server has ever produced.

> *"It was never about the grass or the mycelium. It was about the principle."*
> — Grian

## The Two Sides

### Hermitcraft Environmental Protection (HEP)
**Led by:** GoodTimesWithScar

HEP was an organisation dedicated to keeping the Shopping District clean, beautiful, and covered in **grass**. Scar served as its charismatic figurehead, cleaning up mycelium patches and deploying wolves to protect the district.

**Notable HEP members:** GoodTimesWithScar, BdoubleO100, FalseSymmetry, Cubfan135, and others.

### The Mycelium Resistance
**Led by:** Grian

A secretive underground movement whose stated mission was to reintroduce **mycelium** into the Shopping District — "for the principle." The Resistance operated covertly, planting mycelium patches (sometimes with a Mycelium Resistance sign) and building a secret underground headquarters.

**Notable Resistance members:** Grian, MumboJumbo, TangoTek, ZombieCleo, xBCrafted, iJevin, ImpulseSV.

**Recruitment:** xBCrafted served as **Head of Recruitment**, successfully enlisting iJevin and MumboJumbo into the cause.

## Key Events

### The Sheep Strategy
Grian attempted to spread mycelium by breeding sheep in the Shopping District — since sheep eat grass but not mycelium, the ground would naturally convert. Scar countered by releasing HEP wolves, which attacked and killed most of the sheep.

### The Secret HQ
The Mycelium Resistance built a hidden underground headquarters beneath the Shopping District — one of the season's best-kept secrets for some time.

### The Arrests
HEP eventually captured the entire Mycelium Resistance. With all members captured, both sides agreed to settle the conflict through a **minigame competition**.

## Resolution: The Turf War Minigames
Rather than conventional combat, the war was decided by a series of custom minigames:

- **HEP** built 4 minigames
- **Mycelium Resistance** built 5 minigames
- Final score: **Mycelium Resistance 6 – HEP 3**

**The Mycelium Resistance won.** As agreed, HEP was required to restore the Shopping District — but the victory was ultimately bittersweet and philosophical. In the end, it really never was about the grass or mycelium.

## Legacy

The Mycelium Resistance vs HEP is widely considered **the best server-wide storyline in Hermitcraft history**. It combined:
- A beautifully absurd premise
- Genuine tension and stakes within the game world
- Cross-Hermit collaboration on both sides
- Meme-able moments ("for the principle")
- A satisfying narrative arc with a definitive resolution

The phrase *"it was never about the [X], it was about the principle"* became a lasting community meme.
