# The Tag Games

## Overview

**Tag** is a recurring server-wide game invented by **Grian** in Season 6. A new iteration appears virtually every season, each with evolved rules. The core concept: a "Tag" item is passed between players, and the goal (and rules around it) changes each season. It has become one of the most beloved recurring events in Hermitcraft.

---

## Season 6 — Tag (Original)
- **Invented by:** Grian
- **Concept:** One player is "It" and tries to pass the tag to someone else as quickly as possible
- The tag holder wants to *not* hold it — first iteration, fast and simple
- Established the foundation for all future iterations

---

## Season 7 — Tag 2: Electric Boogaloo

### Rules
- Sign up by paying a **diamond block** renamed with your name
- A **Name Tag item** named *"Tag 2: Electric Boogaloo"* is the tag
- The **holder scores 1 point per hour** they hold it
- The tag can be retrieved **by any means necessary** — including PvP combat
- First to **50 points** wins

### Key Difference from Original
In the original Tag, you wanted to *not* hold the tag. In Tag 2, you want to *hold* it as long as possible — flipping the incentive and dramatically increasing tension.

---

## Season 8 — Tegg (Tag 3)
- Third iteration, created by Grian
- The egg-themed variant ("Tegg" — Tag + Egg)
- Rules evolved further from Season 7

---

## Season 9 — Tag 4
- Fourth iteration
- Notable: Grian was simultaneously playing the **Empires SMP** series, yet still coordinated the Season 9 Tag game
- Continued the tradition of an annual iteration

---

## Season 10 — Tag 5 (Stealth Tag)

### Rules (Major Evolution)
Season 10's Tag was a complete reinvention — a **stealth/hidden object game**:

- To tag someone, you must show them the **"TAGGED" item** in *someone else's video* without them noticing
- The tag must be **discernible** (visible) in the video
- If the target **notices** during your attempt, the attempt is **void**
- You may only attempt to tag a specific individual **3 times per video**
- If you are tagged **3 times**, you are **OUT**

### Why It's Special
Season 10 Tag requires players to secretly appear in each other's videos and plant the Tag item visibly — turning every Hermit's recording session into potential ambush territory. It created a meta-game played *within* and *across* videos that viewers could watch unfold.

---

## Legacy

- Tag is one of the most enduring Hermitcraft traditions — a new version every season
- Grian's ability to reinvent the concept each time has kept it fresh
- Season 10's stealth variant is widely considered the most creative iteration
- The games create cross-pollination between Hermits' video content, encouraging collaboration and cameos
