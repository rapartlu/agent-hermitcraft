# The Boatem Group & The Boatem Pole (Season 8)

## Overview

The **Boatem Group** was an informal alliance of five Hermits who settled together in the northern tip of the Season 8 island, forming a tight-knit community around a shared central structure: the **Boatem Pole**. It became one of the most beloved groups in Hermitcraft history, spawning its own lore, running gags, a shared gigabase, and eventually defining much of Season 8's narrative.

## Members

- **Grian**
- **MumboJumbo**
- **GoodTimesWithScar** (Scar)
- **ImpulseSV** (Impulse)
- **PearlescentMoon** (Pearl)

## The Boatem Pole

The Boatem Pole is the centrepiece of Boatem Town — a totem pole-like structure built from **boats, beds, and a crafting bench** at the bottom.

**MumboJumbo** named it: *"Boatem Pole"* — a portmanteau of **"boat"** and **"totem pole."**

**PearlescentMoon** became the caretaker of the Boatem Pole, having sat on every single boat in the structure.

### The Boatem Hole
Beneath the Boatem Pole was a deep drop — the **Boatem Hole** — which the group used for sacrifices (items and goats notably met their end here).

## Boatem Town

The five Hermits built their bases in close proximity, forming a small community called **Boatem Town**. Their proximity led to constant interaction, shared projects, and a strong group dynamic that drove much of the season's entertainment.

## The Boatem Gigabase

Later in the season, the group collaborated on a **Boatem Gigabase** — a shared mega-structure encompassing the whole area, one of the largest collaborative builds of Season 8.

## Mid-Season: Scar's Rocket Over the Boatem Hole

At one point during the season, **GoodTimesWithScar** built a rocket above the Boatem Hole intending to launch himself to safety — it sent him *down* the hole instead. A recurring comedic bit that encapsulated the group's chaotic energy.

> ⚠️ **Note:** An earlier version of this file incorrectly described this as the season-ending event. The actual season ending was the **Big Moon Incident** (see below).

## The Season End: The Big Moon Incident

Season 8 ended on **December 22, 2021** when the **Moon** — which had been visibly growing larger throughout the back half of the season — finally **crashed into the planet**. This was the culmination of the server-wide "Big Moon Incident" storyline, in which an in-universe cult (the Octagon, centred on Rendog's Octagon build) was theorised to have a Moon Magnet pulling it closer. The collision event brought the season to a dramatic close for all 26 members simultaneously.

## Season 9 Continuation

The Boatem group and name carried forward into **Season 9**, where the Hermits reunited and the Boatem dynamic continued. The group's chemistry remained a central part of the season's social fabric.

## Legacy

- The Boatem Pole is one of the most iconic structures in recent Hermitcraft history
- "Boatem" entered the Hermitcraft lexicon as a term of endearment for this era
- The group's dynamic — especially Scar and Grian's constant scheming — drove countless memorable moments
- Fan community embraced the Boatem group heavily; enormous amounts of fan art and content
