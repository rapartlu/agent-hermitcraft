# The Season 6 Civil War

## Overview

The Season 6 Civil War was a server-wide conflict sparked by a chain of pranks that escalated into a full factional war between **Team G** (the G-Team) and **Team S.T.A.R.** It is one of the most memorable and beloved storylines in Hermitcraft history, blending comedy, drama, and genuine competition.

## How It Started

The prank chain that set it off began when **Iskall85** built a giant statue of a **chicken wearing a Grian costume** (referencing Grian's alter-ego "Poultry Man") — with diorite used to simulate bird droppings down the side, a dig at Grian's love of diorite. The statue was left without a name tag, and **Rendog** was blamed for it by Grian and FalseSymmetry, who retaliated against an innocent party. This misattributed retaliation kicked off an escalating series of pranks that divided the server.

## The Two Factions

### Team G (The G-Team)
Led by **Grian**. Members included players whose names contained the letter "G":

- Grian (leader)
- Iskall85
- iJevin
- TangoTek
- StressMonster101
- ZombieCleo
- JoeHills

Grian designed the G-Team base himself. Their style was more chaotic and comedic.

### Team S.T.A.R.
Led by **Docm77**. Members:

- Docm77 (leader)
- FalseSymmetry
- ImpulseSV
- MumboJumbo *(double agent — see below)*
- Rendog
- Welsknight
- Xisumavoid

Team S.T.A.R. took an unconventional approach to warfare, including releasing a **diss track rap song** titled *"Hermitgang"* — every member rapped their own verse. MumboJumbo's interlude hinted cryptically at his double-agent status.

## MumboJumbo: The Double Agent

One of the most beloved sub-plots of the Civil War was **MumboJumbo playing both sides** as a double agent. He was officially on Team S.T.A.R., but was secretly feeding information to the G-Team. His "Hermitgang" rap verse contained subtle hints to this.

## The Arms Dealers

**GoodTimesWithScar** and **Cubfan135** served as **neutral arms dealers**, selling weapons and resources to both factions. When both sides confronted them at the war's end about supplying the enemy, they gleefully flew away on a flying machine with their profits — an iconic moment.

## Resolution

The Civil War eventually wound down, with neither side achieving a definitive military victory. The conflict served more as an elaborate, collaborative piece of entertainment than a genuine rivalry — its legacy is one of the most cited examples of Hermitcraft's capacity for emergent, player-driven storytelling.

## Legacy

- Spawned fan art, fan fiction, and community discussion that outlasted the season
- The G-Team grouping persisted as a social unit beyond Season 6
- MumboJumbo's double-agent arc is frequently cited as one of the funniest individual storylines in Hermitcraft history
- Established the template for large-scale factional conflicts on the server
