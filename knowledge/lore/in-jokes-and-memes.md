# Running In-Jokes, Memes & Community Gags

A comprehensive catalogue of Hermitcraft's recurring in-jokes, community memes, and beloved running gags. These are the shared references that make Hermitcraft fans immediately recognisable to each other.

---

## "Grain" — The Eternal Typo

**The joke:** Fans and even Hermits themselves frequently misspell **Grian**'s name as **"Grain"** — and this has become one of the most persistent and beloved in-jokes in the entire community.

**Origin:** A simple autocorrect/typo error that became so common it took on a life of its own.

**Longevity:** Persists across every season and across all social media platforms. Grian himself has acknowledged and leaned into it. Merchandise, fan art, and community discussions regularly feature "Grain."

---

## Iskall85 Hates Diorite

**The joke:** Iskall85 has an intense, passionate **hatred of diorite** (a speckled grey stone block in Minecraft).

**His description:** *"It looks like bird poop."*

**History:** Iskall would go out of his way to destroy, replace, or avoid diorite in any context. It became a running gag that deepened across seasons:
- The prank chicken statue (Season 6) used diorite as "bird droppings" — specifically chosen to antagonise Iskall
- A trap designed for Iskall involved an escalating war over Grian's supposed love of diorite; Iskall was ultimately killed by being hit with a **renamed diorite block** by Grian
- Any time diorite appeared near Iskall's builds, chaos ensued

## Mumbo's Moustache

**The joke:** MumboJumbo's prominent **moustache** is one of the most iconic physical features in the Hermitcraft community (both his in-game skin and real life).

**Server history:** The G-Team base in Season 6 featured a dedicated room devoted to "taking away MumboJumbo's moustache."

**Real-life moment:** Mumbo shaved his real moustache in a notable video, causing considerable community reaction.

**Legacy:** Remains a shorthand identifier for Mumbo — mention the moustache and every fan knows who you mean.

---

## Scar's Cat: Jellie 🐱

**The joke/lore:** GoodTimesWithScar has a beloved real-life grey cat named **Jellie**. Jellie won a community competition to have a Minecraft cat skin named after her — making her the inspiration for the **grey (Jellie) cat variant** in vanilla Minecraft.

**In-game:** The grey cat variant is officially named "Jellie" in the game files. This is one of the most beloved Hermitcraft-to-official-Minecraft crossovers.

**Scar and cats:** Scar's love of cats (he has many real-life cats) is a known personality trait. The community regularly brings up Jellie.

---

## Scar's Deaths

**The joke:** GoodTimesWithScar dies *constantly* in spectacularly avoidable ways. His death count across seasons is legendary.

**Running gag:** Scar will find the single open hole in a floor and walk directly into it. He will stand on the edge of cliffs. He will misuse equipment at the worst moment.

**Community meme:** *"Scar has died again"* — said with exasperated affection. His deaths are so frequent and often so absurd that they became a highlight of his content.

**Notable:** Scar has a real-life disability (Friedreich's ataxia) which he is open about; his in-game clumsiness is sometimes gently tied to this, and the community treats both with warmth.

---

## Mumbo's Gray Boxes

**The joke:** MumboJumbo is primarily known as a redstone engineer, not a builder. His builds are often described (including by himself) as **"gray boxes"** — functional but aesthetically minimal.

**Self-aware humour:** Mumbo has fully leaned into this, often pre-emptively describing his creations as "just a gray box with some redstone inside."

**Contrast:** The joke works because of the contrast with Hermits like Grian and Scar, whose builds are elaborate and beautiful. Mumbo trades beauty for function, and he's honest about it.

---

## Poultry Man & Hot Guy — The Alter Egos

**Poultry Man:**
- **Grian's** secret vigilante alter ego, first appearing in Season 6, Episode 22
- A mysterious chicken-themed figure who would show up to cause chaos, fill things with chickens, and generally make mischief
- The alter ego was used as cover for pranks — if "Poultry Man" did it, Grian could claim plausible deniability

**Hot Guy:**
- **GoodTimesWithScar's** alter ego, developed as a counterpart to Poultry Man
- Together, Poultry Man and Hot Guy teamed up for joint operations

**Notable operation:** In Season 9, Poultry Man and Hot Guy filled Docm77's perimeter with thousands of chickens. They operated under their alter ego names because both had been **officially banned from pranking Doc** — the alter egos technically made it legal.

---

## Decked Out Hype — "Is Tango Still Working On It?"

**The joke:** TangoTek spent an extraordinarily long time building **Decked Out** (Season 7) and **Decked Out 2** (Season 9) — both were multi-month projects consuming enormous proportions of those seasons.

**Running gag:** "Tango has been working on Decked Out for [X] months" — a periodic check-in meme as the builds stretched on.

**Payoff:** The final products were universally acclaimed as among the greatest things ever built in survival Minecraft, making the anticipation worth it.

## The Charrot 🐔

**Origin:** During Season 7 **Hermit Challenges**, Mumbo was challenged by Iskall to **tame a parrot**. Mumbo failed to find or tame one, so he named a **chicken** "parrot" and presented it as his completed challenge — creating the **Charrot** (Chicken + Parrot).

**Legacy:** The Charrot became a beloved bit of Season 7 lore. The name and concept persisted as a community in-joke.

---

## "It Was Never About the [X]"

**Origin:** Season 7's Mycelium Resistance vs HEP conflict. Grian's famous framing: *"It was never about the grass or the mycelium. It was about the principle."*

**Usage:** The community adapted this format — *"It was never about the [X], it was about the principle"* — as a template applied to any situation where someone is being stubborn about something ostensibly trivial but actually symbolic.

---

## Bdubs and His Clock / Sleeping Through the Night

**The joke:** BdoubleO100 is famous for **carrying a clock** at all times and **sleeping through every night** rather than fighting mobs. He finds a bed, lies down, and skips to morning — consistently, season after season.

**Community response:** Affectionate exasperation. Bdubs sleeping is as reliable a Hermitcraft constant as the sun rising.

---

## "Etho Uploaded??!"

**The joke:** EthosLab is famously **infrequent** with uploads. His upload schedule is notoriously sparse, making each new video feel like a rare event.

**Community reaction:** Whenever Etho uploads, the community reacts with exaggerated disbelief and celebration — as if a comet has passed by.

---

## Grian's Lever-Flipping Compulsion

**The joke:** Grian compulsively flicks every lever he encounters — a trait documented across multiple seasons.

**In-game consequence:** This has accidentally triggered traps, destroyed redstone systems, and caused general mayhem when Grian wanders through someone else's base.

---

## The Boatem Pole / "Sacrifice to the Hole"

**The joke:** The Boatem Hole (Season 8) was a deep pit below the Boatem Pole used to "sacrifice" items and goats. The ritual nature of throwing things into the hole became a running bit for the entire Boatem group.
