# Hermit Challenges & Major Collaborations

## Hermit Challenges (Season 7+)

**Hermit Challenges** is a recurring mini-game framework started by **MumboJumbo** in his Season 7, Episode 2. It formalises the long-standing tradition of Hermits betting and daring each other.

### Core Players (Season 7)
- MumboJumbo
- Iskall85
- Grian
- StressMonster101

### How It Works
1. To join, a new member must complete initiation tasks at the **initiation site** (ringing a bell, eating a renamed cookie)
2. Members use **"burnsticks"** to signal they want to be challenged
3. A member can also approach another directly and propose a duel of challenges
4. Challenges are issued creatively — via renamed chickens, written notes, or other in-world means
5. Both parties exchange challenges simultaneously, and a **timeframe** is set for completion

### Notable Challenges

**Iskall vs Mumbo (Season 7 opening):**
- Iskall's challenge: mine **10,000 blocks**
- Mumbo's challenge: **tame a parrot**
- Iskall completed his by building a slime farm (efficient)
- Mumbo **failed** — couldn't find or tame a parrot, so he named a chicken "parrot" instead → the birth of the **Charrot** (see in-jokes file)

The Charrot became one of Season 7's most beloved recurring bits.

### Decked Out (Season 7) — TangoTek
**Participant:** TangoTek (creator); all Hermits as players

A fully-featured **dungeon crawler card game** built entirely in Survival Minecraft. Players used physical card items to navigate a dungeon filled with traps, mob spawners, and objectives. Featured:
- A physical card deck system
- Dungeon rooms with escalating difficulty
- Leaderboards and competitive runs
- Server-wide participation

Widely considered **one of the greatest single builds in Minecraft history**.

---

### Decked Out 2 (Season 9) — TangoTek
**Participant:** TangoTek (creator); all Hermits as players

The sequel, even more elaborate than the original. Built over years, it consumed much of Season 9. The community watched it develop in real time across dozens of TangoTek episodes — the wait and the reveal were both legendary.

**Participants:** Grian, MumboJumbo, GoodTimesWithScar + many others

## Minecraft Championship (MCC) Participation

Many Hermits regularly participate in **MC Championship** — a large-scale Minecraft competitive event run by Noxcrew, featuring ~40 players divided into teams competing across multiple mini-games.

### Notable Hermit MCC Achievements
- **FalseSymmetry** is the most decorated MCC competitor among Hermits — won **four times** (MCC 9, MCC 10, MCC 17, MCC 29), the first player to achieve multiple consecutive wins
- Multiple Hermits participate per event, often introducing their audiences to other MCYT creators

---

## Weekly Hermitcraft Meetings

A recurring in-universe institution: **weekly Hermitcraft meetings** where all online Hermits gather (usually at the shopping district or a community space) to discuss server plans, propose events, settle disputes, and pitch ideas.

**Notable use:** The perimeter terraforming prank was officially pitched (and approved) at a weekly meeting — lending it the air of a democratically sanctioned prank.

These meetings are a significant part of Hermitcraft's communal governance and appear in many Hermits' videos.
