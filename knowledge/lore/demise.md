# Demise

## Overview

**Demise** is a recurring Hermitcraft server-wide survival game, first introduced by Grian in **Season 6**. The core concept: be the **last Hermit to die**. No PvP — every death comes from normal survival gameplay. The longer you survive, the more tension mounts.

It has run in Season 6 and Season 10, with variations in rules each time.

---

## Season 6 Demise

### Rules
- Goal: be the last player alive
- **No PvP kills** allowed — deaths must come from the environment, mobs, accidents, etc.
- Cost **50 diamonds** to sign up
- Participants could **not wear diamond armour** (but could enchant lesser armour freely)
- Player skins turned monochrome upon death

### Timeline
- **Started:** October 15, 2019 (12:00 UTC)
- **Ended:** December 24, 2019

### Winner
**Iskall85** — the last surviving participant.

Iskall won when **Docm77** (the second-to-last player alive) died in his Season 6, Episode 136.

### Notable Moments
- The game ran for over two months, creating constant background tension across every Hermit's videos
- Players became increasingly paranoid and cautious as the field narrowed
- Deaths were often accidental and spectacular — mobs, falls, drowning, fire

---

## Season 10 Demise

### Rules (Updated)
Season 10 added the **Reaper mechanic**, adding an active hunting element:

- Goal: be the last player alive
- Upon death, a Hermit becomes a **Reaper** — tasked with killing another Hermit by means *other than direct PvP* (can't just punch them; must use traps, tricks, etc.)
- Once a Reaper successfully kills someone, they join the **Dead team** (and stop hunting)
- All 27 Hermits participated from day one — no sign-up required
- Instead of monochrome skins, eliminated players' **usernames turned red**

### Winner
**FalseSymmetry** — the last living player.

Her winning ceremony was memorable: she was **voluntarily killed** by MumboJumbo, Xisumavoid, StressMonster101, and xBCrafted, who placed dispensers around her and set them off simultaneously — a ceremonial end to the game.

### Prize
The **Hermit Permit** — a pass granting the right to sell a specific item in the Season 10 Shopping District.

FalseSymmetry received a **Joker Permit**: the right to sell *any item of her choice*, but once chosen, it could not be changed.

### Connection to Shopping District
The distribution of Hermit Permits for the Season 10 Shopping District was tied to Demise results — adding real in-game stakes beyond just bragging rights.

---

## Legacy

- Demise is one of Hermitcraft's most beloved recurring events
- The format — normal survival with a deadly meta-game overlay — creates natural drama without requiring explicit conflict
- The Reaper mechanic in Season 10 added a creative, social engineering layer
- FalseSymmetry's ceremonial execution at the end of Season 10 Demise is a fan-favourite moment
