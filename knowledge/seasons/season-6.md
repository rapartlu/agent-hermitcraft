# Hermitcraft Season 6

## Overview

Season 6 launched on **July 19, 2018** and ran for nearly 19 months — at the time the longest season. It is widely regarded as the **most-watched Hermitcraft season** and the one that brought the largest mainstream audience. The season featured corporate capitalism satire (ConCorp vs Sahara), a full-scale factional Civil War, and the first running of **Demise**.

## Minecraft Version

**1.13** at launch; settled on **1.14.4** for most of the season. Never updated to 1.15.

Not publicly disclosed. JoeHills hinted on Twitter (July 15, 2018) that the seed has four prime factors, one of which is **1,409,558,884,499**. Full seed never released.

## Key Themes

- Rival **corporations** (ConCorp vs Sahara) as the economic meta-game
- **Civil War** (G-Team vs Team S.T.A.R.) as the server-wide conflict
- Themed world layout — large island with 7 building districts surrounding Aqua Town
- **Demise** — first running of the last-Hermit-standing survival game

## Notable Events

- **Civil War (G-Team vs S.T.A.R.)** — Grian-led G-Team vs Docm77-led S.T.A.R.; MumboJumbo as double agent; Scar & Cub as neutral arms dealers; Team S.T.A.R. recorded the "Hermitgang" rap. See `knowledge/lore/civil-war-season6.md`
- **Sahara** — Grian, MumboJumbo, and Iskall85 (the Architechs) built a fully automated shopping complex as a rival to ConCorp
- **ConCorp** — GoodTimesWithScar and Cubfan135's dominant shopping monopoly
- **Demise** — Oct 15 – Dec 24, 2019; Iskall85 won (last survivor); cost 50 diamonds to enter; no diamond armour allowed
- Season 6 drove the largest viewership of any Hermitcraft season to date

## Major Builds

- **Sahara** automated shopping complex (Grian, MumboJumbo, Iskall85)
- **ConCorp** retail empire (Scar, Cub)
- **Area 77** theme park
- Shopping district with 7 themed building zones
- G-Team and S.T.A.R. faction bases and battlefield
