# Hermitcraft Season 7

## Overview

Season 7 launched on **February 28, 2020** — just as COVID-19 lockdowns began worldwide, contributing to record viewership. It ran for 15+ months and is home to two of the most celebrated storylines in Hermitcraft history: the **Mycelium Resistance vs HEP turf war** and **TangoTek's Decked Out** dungeon-crawler minigame.

## Key Themes

- Era of **mega-base building** — every member built a large, ambitious individual base
- **Factional conflict**: Mycelium Resistance (Grian) vs HEP / Hermitcraft Environmental Protection (Scar)
- **Decked Out** — TangoTek's dungeon-crawler card game, celebrated as one of the greatest Hermitcraft community builds
- **Head Games** — server-wide head-collecting economy minigame

## Notable Events

- **Mycelium Resistance vs HEP** — Grian spread mycelium in the shopping district; Scar formed HEP to contain it; the Resistance went underground, used sheep as stealth carriers, eventually won via a series of 9 mini-games. See `knowledge/lore/mycelium-resistance-season7.md`
- **Decked Out** — TangoTek built a full dungeon-crawler with custom cards, loot, and leaderboards inside the shopping district. Players collected artifact sets across runs
- **1.16 Nether Reset** (June 23, 2020) — the Nether Update prompted a new Nether and renewed exploration
- **Head Games** — a server-wide head-collecting minigame that drove player interaction
- **Among Us events** — inspired by the game's massive popularity during lockdowns

## Major Builds

- **Decked Out dungeon** (TangoTek) in the Shopping District
- MumboJumbo's Parliament / Government building
- Grian's Midnight Alley
- 24 individual mega-bases — widely considered the best individual build quality of any Hermitcraft season
