## Key Milestones

- **Season 1 (2012):** Server founded by Generikb; Xisumavoid takes over as admin
- **Season 3 (2014):** EthosLab, Docm77, ImpulseSV join; Amplified world; seed publicly known
- **Season 4 (2016):** "Kingdomcraft Five" join (Scar, Iskall, Cub, Rendog, Welsknight); VintageBeef joins
- **Season 5 (2017):** StressMonster101 and Zedaph join
- **Season 6 (2018):** Grian joins; Civil War; Sahara; first Demise; largest viewership
- **Season 7 (2020):** No new members (first time); Mycelium Resistance; Decked Out 1; COVID lockdown viewership peak
- **Season 8 (2021):** GeminiTay and PearlescentMoon join; shortest season; Big Moon Incident ends it
- **Season 9 (2022):** First season with zero roster changes; Decked Out 2; longest season
- **Season 10 (2024):** SmallishBeans and Skizzleman join; Demise with Reapers; Hermit Permits
- **Season 11 (2025):** 25 returning; Decked Out 3; ongoing
