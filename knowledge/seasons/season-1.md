# Hermitcraft Season 1

## Overview

Season 1 was the founding season of Hermitcraft, launched on **April 13, 2012** by **Generikb**. Starting with just 10 members, the server grew substantially throughout the season as Generikb and later Xisumavoid invited new players. The season ran for approximately 14 months.

At the close of Season 1, Generikb announced he was leaving the server. **Xisumavoid** stepped up to take over as server administrator — a role he has held ever since.
