# Hermitcraft Season 10

## Overview

Season 10 launched in **late January / early February 2024** on Minecraft **1.20.4**, featuring the largest roster in several seasons with **27 members** — including two new players, SmallishBeans and Skizzleman. The season ran through multiple Minecraft version updates and introduced a revamped **Demise** game with a new **Reaper mechanic** and tied the outcome to the Shopping District's **Hermit Permit** economy.

## Key Themes

- **Demise with Reaper mechanic** — returned after Season 6, now with active hunting roles post-death
- **Hermit Permits** — a structured economy giving each Hermit the right to sell specific items in the Shopping District
- New friendship groups: **ZITS** (Zedaph, ImpulseSV, TangoTek, Skizzleman) and **Poe-Poe** (GoodTimesWithScar, Grian, Cubfan135, Skizzleman)
- New **Tag** game iteration

## Notable Events

- **Demise (Season 10)** — all 27 Hermits participated from day one; eliminated players became **Reapers** tasked with killing another using traps (not direct PvP). **FalseSymmetry** won; her ceremonial end was memorable. See `knowledge/lore/demise.md`
- **Hermit Permits** — each Hermit received a permit to sell a specific item in the Shopping District; permit distribution was tied to Demise results. FalseSymmetry's Joker Permit allowed any item
- **Skizzleman and SmallishBeans** join as new Hermits and integrate quickly into established groups
- New **Tag** game run by Grian (see `knowledge/lore/tag-games.md`)

## Major Builds

- Shopping District with the Hermit Permit economy system
- ZITS group collaborative projects
- Poe-Poe group builds (Scar, Grian, Cub, Skizzleman)
- Individual mega-bases
