# Hermitcraft Season 5

## Overview

Season 5 launched around **April 2017** on Minecraft **1.11**. It was a relatively open, exploration-focused season without a single dominant server-wide narrative. Two new members joined: **StressMonster101** and **Zedaph**. The season ended in mid-2018 when Minecraft 1.13 significantly changed world generation, making a fresh start preferable to continuing.
