# Hermitcraft Season 4

## Overview

Season 4 launched in early 2016 on Minecraft **1.9** and introduced a major roster expansion. **Five players from the Kingdomcraft server** joined together — GoodTimesWithScar, Iskall85, Cubfan135, Rendog, and Welsknight — all recommended by PythonGB. VintageBeef also joined this season. The world featured themed residential and entertainment districts for the first time.

## Key Themes

- First season with formally structured **themed districts**:
  - North and South Residential Districts
  - Orange Entertainment District (The Danglands)
  - Yellow Farming District
- The "Kingdomcraft Five" joining dramatically expanded the roster
- 1.9 combat overhaul changed PvP and combat dynamics

## Notable Events

- **GoodTimesWithScar, Iskall85, Cubfan135, Rendog, Welsknight** all join together — this group would shape the server's culture for many seasons
- **EthosLab and Docm77** built **The Danglands** — a minigame district featuring Whack-A-Mole, Boat Racing, The Fat Princess, and Connect 4
- **5K Club** — a club for players who had walked 5,000 blocks from spawn, established by FalseSymmetry, Cubfan135, and GoodTimesWithScar
- PythonGB (who recommended the Kingdomcraft Five) participated as a member this season

## Major Builds

- **The Danglands** (Docm77 + EthosLab) — minigame entertainment district
- Individual mega-bases across the residential districts
- The world's first formally planned shopping/building zone layout
