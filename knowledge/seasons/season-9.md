# Hermitcraft Season 9

## Overview

Season 9 launched on **March 5, 2022** on Minecraft **1.18.1** using the **Fabric mod loader**, and ran until **December 20, 2023** — making it the **longest Hermitcraft season** at 21+ months. All 26 Season 8 members returned and no new players joined or left, the first time in server history with zero roster changes. The season's defining project was **Decked Out 2** — the sequel to Season 7's celebrated dungeon-crawler.

## Key Themes

- **Decked Out 2** — TangoTek's massively expanded sequel dungeon-crawler, building on Season 7's original
- **Boatem group continues** — the Season 8 group reunited and maintained their dynamic
- Prank wars and player-vs-player competition
- **Soup Group** — ImpulseSV, PearlescentMoon, and GeminiTay's collaboration group
- Longest season in Hermitcraft history

## Notable Events

- **Decked Out 2** — TangoTek spent much of the season building an enormously expanded version of Decked Out; widely considered one of the greatest individual builds in Minecraft history
- **Soup Group** (ImpulseSV, PearlescentMoon, GeminiTay) — a close collaboration group with a shared base aesthetic
- **The Boatem group continues** — the Season 8 chemistry carried over into new builds and projects
- Large-scale prank wars (particularly involving Grian and others)

## Major Builds

- **Decked Out 2** (TangoTek) — a multi-room dungeon-crawler with custom card systems, resource management, and competitive leaderboards; TangoTek spent 11+ months building it
- Shared Boatem/friendship group bases
- Soup Group collaborative projects
