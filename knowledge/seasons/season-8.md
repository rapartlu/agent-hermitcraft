# Hermitcraft Season 8

## Overview

Season 8 launched on **June 19, 2021** and ended on **December 22, 2021** — the **shortest vanilla Hermitcraft season** at ~6 months. The short duration was deliberate: Mojang had split the Caves & Cliffs update in two (1.17 / 1.18), and the Hermits chose a focused experimental season rather than wait for 1.18. The season's defining arc was the **Big Moon Incident** — a server-wide narrative about the Moon growing larger until it crashed into the planet, ending the season.

## Key Themes

- **Shorter experimental season** — deliberately self-contained before the 1.18 world reset
- **Boatem group** — Grian, MumboJumbo, Scar, ImpulseSV, and PearlescentMoon form a tight community around the Boatem Pole
- **Big Moon Incident** — server-wide apocalyptic storyline with the Moon growing larger each week
- Collaborative base groups and shared mega-projects

## Notable Events

- **The Big Moon Incident** — the Moon was observed growing larger starting ~October 21, 2021; factions formed (the Octagon cult vs. scientists); on December 22, 2021, the Moon crashed into the planet, ending the season
- **The Boatem Group** — Grian, MumboJumbo, Scar, ImpulseSV, and PearlescentMoon settled together in the north; named after the "Boatem Pole" (a totem pole of boats). See `knowledge/lore/boatem-season8.md`
- **The Octagon** — Rendog's octagonal structure, became the centre of the Moon-worship cult in-lore
- **MumboJumbo ran for server mayor** and won via a diamond donation campaign
- GeminiTay and PearlescentMoon join — both become long-term core Hermits

## Major Builds

- **Boatem Town** — the shared northern base area for the 5 Boatem members
- **The Octagon / Octatown** (Rendog) — large octagonal walker structure on a dead coral reef
- **Boatem Gigabase** — the group's collaborative mega-structure
- **Big Eyes Crew base** — BdoubleO100, Keralis, and TangoTek in a shared spruce forest base
