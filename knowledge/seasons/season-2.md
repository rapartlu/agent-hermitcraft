# Hermitcraft Season 2

## Overview

Season 2 began around **June 2013** after Generikb's departure at the end of Season 1. **Xisumavoid**, now fully in charge, used the reset as an opportunity to rebuild and expand the community, whitelisting many new players. The season ran until October 2014 when Season 3 launched on Minecraft 1.8.

## Key Themes

- Community rebuilding after Generikb's departure
- Xisumavoid's leadership era begins

- Xisumavoid formally takes over as server admin and server identity
- MumboJumbo joins — becomes one of the server's most prominent redstone builders
