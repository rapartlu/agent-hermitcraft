# VintageBeef

## Overview

VintageBeef is a Canadian Minecraft YouTuber who joined Hermitcraft **halfway through Season 4** (2016). Originally a member of the MindCrack server, he is known for unique, creative projects that go beyond standard Minecraft gameplay — including a fully playable trading card game and elaborate map art.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [VintageBeef](https://www.youtube.com/user/VintageBeef)

## Specialty & Playstyle

Beef is known for:
- **Detailed farming and rustic aesthetics** — his bases often have a hand-crafted, organic countryside feel
- **Creative projects** that push survival Minecraft in unusual directions (card games, map art)
- **Attention to detail** in world-building and storytelling through builds

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 4 — Martian base:** Inspired by the movie *The Martian*, built a Martian-themed survival base.
- **Season 7 — White farmhouse:** Built a white farmhouse starter base, then terraformed the surrounding area into a full working farm aesthetic.
- **Season 7 — Wallpaper Shop:** Sold custom-made map art as "wallpaper" in the shopping district.
- **Season 9 — Trading Card Game:** Created a fully playable trading card game inside Minecraft — one of the most creative projects of that season.
- **Season 10 — Big farm base:** Built a large, detailed farm featuring wheat/crop/flower fields, animals (horses, pigs, cows, chickens), an automatic pig slaughter machine, custom birch trees, winding paths, and a custom mail building.

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- Previously a member of **MindCrack** — one of the few Hermits to have been on both iconic servers.
- Known for his calm, relaxed commentary style.
- His **trading card game** in Season 9 was widely praised as a creative tour de force.
