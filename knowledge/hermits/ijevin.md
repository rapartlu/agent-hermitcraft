# iJevin

## Overview

iJevin (known as "Jevin") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 2**, making him one of the longer-serving members. He is known for his building creativity, his pixel art, and his involvement in server-wide events and storylines. His longevity on the server means he is "as close to an original member as one can get" outside the founding cohort.
