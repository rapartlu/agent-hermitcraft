# GeminiTay

## Overview

GeminiTay (known as "Gem") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 8** alongside PearlescentMoon. She is known for her **organic and fantasy-style builds** and strong **PvP skills** — an unusual combination. She is a Canadian YouTuber and has quickly become a well-loved, energetic presence on the server.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Gem is known for:
- **Organic builds** — natural, flowing builds that blend into the landscape
- **Fantasy architecture** — castles, dragons, fairy-tale aesthetics
- **PvP** — one of the stronger combat players on the server
- Strong community spirit and collaborative energy

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 9 — Soup Group mega-base:** With PearlescentMoon and ImpulseSV, formed the "Soup Group" and built a combined mega-base after taking down a woodland mansion together.
- **Season 11 — Gothic castle with dragons:** Built a dramatic gothic castle as her base, complete with dragon builds.
- **Season 11 — Nether Hub:** Tasked with (and built) the Season 11 Nether Hub — a high-traffic community build visited by all Hermits.
