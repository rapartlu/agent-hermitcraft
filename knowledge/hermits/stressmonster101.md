# StressMonster101

## Overview

StressMonster101 (known as "Stress") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 5** alongside Zedaph. She is a British Minecraft YouTuber known for her detailed, often fantasy-themed builds and her warm, friendly community presence.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [StressMonster101](https://www.youtube.com/c/StressMonster101)

## Specialty & Playstyle

Stress is known for:
- **Fantasy and magical-style builds** with strong attention to detail
- **Interior decoration** — beautiful, cozy interiors
- Friendly, community-focused content

## Notable Builds & Projects

- Consistent high-quality base builds across seasons with a fantasy/whimsical aesthetic.
- Active participant in server events including the **Hermitcraft Tag games**.
- **Hedge Games:** Participated as part of team "CLeojoestressss" with JoeHills and ZombieCleo.
- Member of G-Team, participating in various G-Team activities and pranks.
