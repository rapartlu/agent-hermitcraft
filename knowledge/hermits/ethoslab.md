# EthosLab

## Overview

EthosLab (known as "Etho") is a legendary figure in the Minecraft YouTube community, widely credited with inventing many commonly used redstone techniques. He joined Hermitcraft in **Season 3** (March 2015) and has been active in most seasons since, with the exception of Season 6. He is Canadian and keeps his real identity private.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [EthosLab](https://www.youtube.com/user/EthosLab)
- **Known for:** One of Minecraft YouTube's earliest and most influential creators; long-running Let's Play series

## Specialty & Playstyle

Etho is a **redstone and technical Minecraft** legend. He is credited with inventing or popularising many redstone circuits and mechanics that are now standard knowledge. His builds tend to be technically impressive rather than aesthetically grand, and he often focuses on:
- Clever redstone mechanisms
- Unique minigames and interactive builds
- Technical contraptions with elegant solutions

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 4 (with Docm77):** Built **"The Danglands"** — a minigame district featuring Whack-A-Mole, Boat Racing, The Fat Princess, and Connect 4.
- **Season 7:** Built an unconventional base without exterior walls in the jungle biome.
- **Season 10:** Built a fully functional **Frogger arcade game** within Hermitcraft — a standout technical achievement.

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- His **sleeping schedule** and infrequent uploads are a long-running community joke ("Etho uploaded?!").
- One of the most respected technical Minecraft creators globally; called the "father of redstone YouTube" by some fans.
- Known for his calm, methodical commentary style.
- Previously a member of the **MindCrack** server before Hermitcraft.
