# TangoTek

## Overview

TangoTek joined Hermitcraft in **Season 2** (July 2014) in an unusual way: MumboJumbo tried to build one of Tango's iron farm designs on the server but couldn't get it to work, so the server whitelisted Tango for a day to fix it — and he never left. He is one of six Hermits to have played **10 seasons** of Hermitcraft.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [TangoTek](https://www.youtube.com/user/TangoTekLP)
- **Known for:** Redstone engineering, elaborate minigames, technical Minecraft builds

## Hermitcraft History

Active across Seasons 2 through 11, making him one of the most tenured members.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Tango is known for two distinct specialties:
1. **Technical Minecraft / Iron Farms** — his iron farm designs (the Iron Foundry, the Iron Titan) are among the most famous in Minecraft's history.
2. **Elaborate Minigame Design** — he creates massive, complex games *inside* Survival Minecraft, which has become his defining creative output.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Decked Out (Season 7):** A fully-featured dungeon crawler card game built in Survival Minecraft — widely considered one of the greatest Minecraft creations ever made. Players used cards to navigate a dungeon with traps, mobs, and objectives.
- **Decked Out 2 (Season 9):** A sequel to Decked Out, even more elaborate, built over years. Became a server-wide event that consumed much of Season 9.
- **Decked Out 3 (Season 11):** Third iteration, continuing the series.
- **Among Us (Hermitcraft version):** Built a fully functional version of the game Among Us inside Minecraft.
- **Boombox / The Tangler:** Additional minigame creations.
- **Iron farms:** Famous farm designs used throughout the community.
