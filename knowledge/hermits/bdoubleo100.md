# BdoubleO100

## Overview

BdoubleO100 (commonly known as "Bdubs") is one of Hermitcraft's **founding members**, having joined on January 5, 2013, alongside Juicetra, Skyzm, and Pungence. He has been an active member of virtually every season of Hermitcraft, making him one of the most tenured Hermits on the server.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [BdoubleO100](https://www.youtube.com/user/bdoubleo)
- **Known for:** Detailed building, interior decorating, medieval/fantasy builds

## Hermitcraft History

One of the seven-or-so original Season 1 members. Has been active across essentially all vanilla seasons.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Bdubs is known as one of Hermitcraft's finest **builders**, with particular strengths in:
- **Interior design** — known for incredibly detailed interiors
- **Medieval and fantasy architecture**
- A tendency to carry a clock and sleep through the night rather than fight mobs (a long-running joke)

His building style emphasises detail, texture, and cozy/lived-in aesthetics.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 7:** Built a **massive castle on a cliff** overlooking a hand-crafted medieval village landscape — one of the most celebrated bases of that season. Partnered with Impulse on various builds.
- **Season 8:** Built on the one-chunk island layout of Season 8.
- **Season 9:** Collaborated with ImpulseSV on a large **cyberpunk city** as their combined mega-base and gaming district.
- **Season 10:** Active, continuing signature detailed builds.
- **Multiple seasons:** Built the Town Hall in the shopping district alongside Grian and GoodTimesWithScar.

## Teams & Groups

- **Big Eyes Crew:** BdoubleO100, TangoTek, Keralis
- Regular collaborator with ImpulseSV across multiple seasons

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- His **clock-carrying / sleeping at night** habit is one of his most iconic traits.
- Famous for shouting "BDUBS!" in a distinctive way in his videos.
- Known for incredibly detailed **interior decoration** — often the best on the server.
- His building commentary style ("gorgeous", "beautiful", "oh my goodness") is iconic and much-loved.
