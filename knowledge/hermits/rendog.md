# Rendog

## Overview

Rendog (known as "Ren") joined Hermitcraft at the start of **Season 4** alongside GoodTimesWithScar, Iskall85, Cubfan135, and Welsknight. He is known for his vivid personality, flair for roleplay and storytelling, and his ability to build strong collaborative relationships with other Hermits. He has a distinctive South African / British accent.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [Rendog](https://www.youtube.com/c/Rendog)

## Specialty & Playstyle

Ren's strengths are:
- **Storytelling and roleplay** — he brings characters and narrative arcs to his Hermitcraft content
- **Building** — particularly large-scale, ambitious structures
- **Collaboration** — a natural partner for other Hermits on big joint projects

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 4–5:** Shared a base with Iskall85; worked on a mushroom island terraforming project turning it into a concrete plastic-look biome.
- **The Octagon (with Docm77):** Built a gigantic antenna on top of the Octagon structure in collaboration with Doc.
- **Various seasons:** Consistently builds elaborate bases with strong lore/narrative framing.

## Teams & Groups

- Part of the **Kingdomcraft Five** who joined together in Season 4 (with Scar, Iskall, Cub, Welsknight)
- Regular collaborator with **Docm77**

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- Known for his boisterous, theatrical personality and memorable in-video moments.
- His **"Ren and Doc"** dynamic is one of the more beloved Hermitcraft friendships.
- Often plays up a "villain" or dramatic character persona in server storylines.
