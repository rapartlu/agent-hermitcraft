# Iskall85

## Overview

Iskall85 joined Hermitcraft at the start of **Season 4** (February 25, 2016) alongside Cubfan135, Welsknight, Rendog, and GoodTimesWithScar — all from the Kingdomcraft server. He has approximately **1.44 million YouTube subscribers** and is known for grand builds, business ventures, and his legendary hatred of diorite.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [iskall85](https://www.youtube.com/user/iskall85)
- **Subscribers:** ~1.44 million

## Specialty & Playstyle

Iskall is a well-rounded player known for:
- **Large-scale building** with impressive scope
- **Redstone engineering** (contributed the redstone systems for Sahara)
- **Ambitious mega-projects** that push the limits of survival Minecraft
- **Business/economic ventures** within the server's player economy

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 4–5:** Set up his starter base with a mushroom island terraforming project (with Rendog); attempted to turn it into a plastic-looking biome using concrete.
- **Sahara (Season 7, with Grian & Mumbo):** Co-built Hermitcraft's Amazon-equivalent mega-shop. Iskall handled the redstone, Grian handled building, and Mumbo... also did redstone. The project was enormous.
- **Sahara Fuel & Sahara Now:** Created spin-off businesses from Sahara, including a chain of "gas stations" (XP, rockets, golden carrots) — Sahara Now was reportedly more profitable than Sahara itself.
- **Season 8:** Built a guardian farm, becoming the server's primary supplier of prismarine and sea lanterns early in the season.

## Teams & Groups

- **Architechs (Season 7):** Iskall85, Grian, MumboJumbo — built Sahara
- **G-Team:** Iskall85, Grian, ZombieCleo, StressMonster101, iJevin, JoeHills, TangoTek

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- His **hatred of diorite** is one of the most well-known personality traits in all of Hermitcraft. His famous quote: *"It looks like bird poop."* He will go out of his way to destroy or replace diorite.
- Known for his Swedish accent and excitable energy.
- The Sahara project is legendary — one of the biggest collaborative builds in server history.
