# Docm77

## Overview

Docm77 (known as "Doc") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 3**. He is renowned as one of the most technically creative and rule-bending players on the server. He is known for working with his "**Hivemind**" — an AI of his own imagination — to push Minecraft's mechanics to their absolute limits, often building contraptions that break conventional design.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [Docm77](https://www.youtube.com/user/docm77)

## Specialty & Playstyle

Doc's specialty is **extreme technical Minecraft** with a focus on:
- **Pushing game mechanics** to unconventional and often absurd extremes
- **Redstone contraptions** that break the expected conventions of each design
- **Perimeter construction** — clearing huge areas of chunks down to bedrock to optimise farm performance
- **Large-scale farms** inside and around the Perimeter

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 4 (with Etho) — The Danglands:** Co-built a minigame district with Whack-A-Mole, Boat Racing, The Fat Princess, and Connect 4.
- **The Perimeter:** Built a massive perimeter (chunk-cleared area) used as the foundation for numerous farms and technical builds.
- **The Octagon:** Built redstone systems inside the Octagon; built a gigantic antenna on top with Rendog; installed an infinite brewing system.
- **Fastest donkey:** Became owner of the server's fastest donkey.
- **Infinite brewing system:** Automated potion production at scale.
- **Season 9 — "Hardest Redstone Build":** Documented building one of his most complex redstone projects.

## Teams & Groups

- Long-time collaborator with **Rendog** on large projects
