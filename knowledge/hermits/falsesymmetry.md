# FalseSymmetry

## Overview

FalseSymmetry (known as "False") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 2** in June 2014, making her one of the longer-serving members of the server. She currently has approximately **445,000 YouTube subscribers**. She is known for her building skills, strong PvP ability, and her lively personality.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [FalseSymmetry](https://www.youtube.com/c/FalseSymmetry)
- **Subscribers:** ~445,000

## Hermitcraft History

Joined Season 2 in June 2014. Her first Hermitcraft episode was a prank episode where she filled the base of KingDaddyDMAC and Hypnotizd with sand and cactus — a memorable debut.

## Specialty & Playstyle

False is known for:
- **Building** — wide range of styles
- **PvP skill** — one of the better combat players on the server
- Energetic, community-focused content

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 2 debut prank:** Filled KingDaddyDMAC and Hypnotizd's base with sand and cactus.
- **5K Club (Season 4):** Co-established the 5K Club with Cubfan135 and GoodTimesWithScar.
- **"Is That Sheep Looking At Me?" (Season 7):** Participated as a contestant in Zedaph's game show — was the first eliminated.
- Active in multiple server-wide events and storylines across all seasons.
