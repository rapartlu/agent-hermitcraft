# Hypnotizd

## Overview

Hypnotizd (known as "Hyp") is one of the **seven original members** of Hermitcraft, invited by founder Generikb when the server launched in Season 1 (2012). The original seven were: Hypnotizd, Kiershar, Xisumavoid, Red3yz, Biffa2001, 777static777, and Sokar. This makes him one of the most tenured Hermits in server history.
