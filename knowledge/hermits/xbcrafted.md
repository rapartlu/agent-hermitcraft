# xBCrafted

## Overview

xBCrafted (known as "xB") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 2**. He is a Canadian Minecraft YouTuber known for his **large-scale building skills** and strong technical knowledge of Minecraft mechanics. He has been a consistent presence on the server for over a decade.
