# Xisumavoid

## Overview

Xisumavoid (known as "Xisuma" or "X") is the **founder and administrator of the Hermitcraft server**. He has been a member since **Season 1** and is one of the seven original members. He took over as server admin after Generikb (the original founder) left. He has approximately **1.84 million YouTube subscribers**.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [xisumavoid](https://www.youtube.com/user/xisumavoid)
- **Subscribers:** ~1.84 million

## Hermitcraft History

One of the original Season 1 members (2012). Has been present and active in every season as both a player and server admin.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Xisuma is known for:
- **Server administration** — manages whitelists, datapacks, plugins, and server upkeep
- **Community farms** — in Season 1 he spent much of his time building community farms for other Hermits
- **Bee persona** — in later seasons he adopted a bee-themed identity ("Beesuma"), incorporating honey and bee-related elements into his builds
- **Futuristic tower farms** — builds large futuristic towers for his farms while finding ways to incorporate honey aesthetics

He was originally known throughout his early Minecraft career as the **"passive mob guy"**.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 1:** Built the majority of community farms for other Hermits.
- **Multiple seasons:** Maintained and updated the server infrastructure, including adding new datapacks and content.
- **"Beesuma" persona:** Adopted in later seasons, leading to bee-themed bases and honey-infused build aesthetics.
- **Asian-themed temple (with Keralis):** Collaborated on a large Asian-inspired temple build.

## Role as Admin

As the server administrator, Xisuma:
- Manages who gets whitelisted (new Hermit invitations)
- Sets up and maintains the server for each new season
- Adds datapacks and technical modifications
- Handles any server-side issues

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- His **bee persona** ("Beesuma") is well-loved by the community.
- As admin, he is the final word on server rules and season resets.
- Known for calm, informative commentary style.
- Produces "Hermitcraft Recap" and update-style content for the community.
