# ZombieCleo

## Overview

ZombieCleo (uses they/them pronouns) joined Hermitcraft during **Season 2** and is one of the server's most creative and distinctive members. They are particularly famous for their mastery of **armor stand manipulation** — using armor stands to create detailed sculptures, characters, and decorative scenes that most players couldn't imagine.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [ZombieCleo](https://www.youtube.com/c/ZombieCleo)

## Hermitcraft History

Joined Season 2, making them one of the longer-serving Hermits. Active across virtually all seasons since.

## Specialty & Playstyle

ZombieCleo's defining specialty is **armor stand art** — the use of positioned and posed armor stands (with and without equipment) to create intricate scenes, characters, and decorative elements. Beyond this, they are known for:
- **Creative, narrative-driven builds**
- **Costume and character aesthetics**
- Builds with strong cultural or historical references

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 11 — Mesoamerican/Aztec base:** Built a Native American Civilizations-themed base with a clear reference to the Aztec city of Tenochtitlan, featuring a **Quetzalcoatl** (feathered serpent deity) as a centrepiece.
- **Armor stand sculptures:** Created throughout multiple seasons — ZombieCleo's armor stand work is widely considered the best on the server.
- Regular participant in G-Team activities and server storylines.
