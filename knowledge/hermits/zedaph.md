# Zedaph

## Overview

ZedaphPlays (known as "Zedaph") joined Hermitcraft in **Season 5** alongside StressMonster101. He is known for his uniquely chaotic, inventive, and often absurd approach to Minecraft — creating wacky contraptions, elaborate game shows, and unconventional builds. He has a well-known love of **sheep**.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [ZedaphPlays](https://www.youtube.com/c/ZedaphPlays)

## Specialty & Playstyle

Zedaph's style is hard to categorise — he does whatever is most interesting or funny, which often involves:
- **Wacky contraptions** with unusual mechanics
- **Game shows and minigames** designed for other Hermits to play
- Creative redstone that serves unconventional purposes

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **"Is That Sheep Looking At Me?" Game Show:** Created a full TV game show on the server for three contestants to play, inspired by a game he built in his single-player world. (FalseSymmetry, Cubfan135, and TangoTek competed; TangoTek won.)
- **Season 7 — Cave of Contraptions:** A cave filled with eccentric inventions including a jump-powered furnace and a bed that randomly decided which side you woke up on.
- **Season 10 — Gaming District:** Ran and built the Gaming District, clearing and constructing the main road.

## Teams & Groups
