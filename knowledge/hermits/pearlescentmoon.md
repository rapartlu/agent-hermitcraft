# PearlescentMoon

## Overview

PearlescentMoon (known as "Pearl") joined Hermitcraft during **Season 8** alongside GeminiTay. She is Australian and has quickly established herself as one of the server's most capable builders and organisers. She is well known for her amazing **building and organisation skills**.
