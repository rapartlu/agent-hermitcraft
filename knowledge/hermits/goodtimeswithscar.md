# GoodTimesWithScar

## Overview

GoodTimesWithScar (commonly known as "Scar") joined Hermitcraft at the start of **Season 4** in February 2016, alongside Cubfan135, Iskall85, Rendog, and Welsknight, all from the Kingdomcraft server. He is best known as the server's premier **terraformer and landscaper**, and is widely regarded as one of the most creative builders on Hermitcraft.

As of 2024–2025, Scar has approximately **2.15 million YouTube subscribers** and **565,000 Twitch followers**.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Scar is the undisputed master of **terraforming** — reshaping the landscape around builds to make them feel organic and cinematic. He builds across a huge range of styles from modern to fantasy, and his videos often feature cinematic timelapses and dramatic storytelling. His content is known for being wholesome and community-focused.

He has a well-known disability (Friedreich's ataxia) and often uses a wheelchair or mobility aids, which he references openly in his content.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 5:** Won the Mayor election, defeating Mumbo by a dramatic margin.
- **Season 7:** Created elaborate terraformed landscapes; collaborated on the Town Hall with Grian and Bdubs.
- **Season 9:** Deep involvement in server lore and storytelling.
- **Season 10:** Built a sweeping, terraformed base with organic natural features.
- **Season 11:** Assigned to build the Shopping District from scratch.
