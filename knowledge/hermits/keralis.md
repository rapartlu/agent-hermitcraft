# Keralis

## Overview

Keralis is a Swedish Minecraft YouTuber who joined Hermitcraft in **Season 1** in May 2012, invited directly by Generikb. He is known for his exceptional building skills — particularly in modern and large-scale architecture — and for his numerous building tutorials. He was **inactive during Seasons 4 and 5** before returning for Season 6.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Keralis is primarily known as a **builder**, specialising in:
- **Modern architecture** — sleek, large-scale modern buildings
- **Variety of styles** — from fantasy to contemporary
- **Building tutorials** — one of his most popular content formats
- Large-scale, detailed structures

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Early seasons:** Settled in a jungle biome and built his first base; later moved to a desert biome for his second base.
- **Asian-themed temple (with Xisumavoid):** Collaborated on a large Asian-inspired temple — a fan-favourite collaborative build.
- **Season 9+ — Air Force Base:** Built in a snowy biome, featuring a full airstrip and mega satellite facility.

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- His **Swedish accent and enthusiasm** make his commentary immediately recognisable.
