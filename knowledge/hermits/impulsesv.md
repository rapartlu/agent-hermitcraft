# ImpulseSV

## Overview

ImpulseSV (Scott V, born March 9, 1981) is an American Minecraft YouTuber who joined Hermitcraft in **Season 3**. He is known for his collaborative spirit, high-quality builds, and focus on taking survival Minecraft to the next level through automation. He is one of the most consistently active members of the server.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [ImpulseSV](https://www.youtube.com/user/impulseSV)
- **Known for:** Hermitcraft builds, automation, Let's Plays, collaborations

## Specialty & Playstyle

Impulse's philosophy is "taking Survival Minecraft to the next level through automation." He focuses on:
- **Large-scale automation and farms**
- **Mega-base construction**, often in collaboration with others
- **Minigame design** (notably a time-trial race/parkour course)

He is a popular collaborator and frequently teams up with other Hermits on shared projects.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 9:** Built a massive **industrial area ("motherboard")** of farms built above and below a flat lime terracotta surface.
- **Season 9 (with Bdubs):** Collaborated on a large **cyberpunk city** serving as a combined mega-base and gaming district.
- **iBuy:** Runs a mega-shop in the Shopping District called "iBuy".
- **iLuminate:** Ran a shop selling light-emitting blocks.
- **Pixelpulse Valley race:** Built a single-player time-trial race and parkour minigame inspired by Ace Race from Minecraft Championship (MCC), which ran for two seasons with prizes for fastest times.
- **Soup Group (Season 9):** Teamed with PearlescentMoon and GeminiTay to build combined mega-bases.
