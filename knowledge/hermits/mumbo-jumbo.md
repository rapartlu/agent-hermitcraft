# MumboJumbo

## Overview

MumboJumbo (real name Oliver Brotherhood) is a British Minecraft YouTuber who joined Hermitcraft during **Season 2** in June 2013. He is one of the server's most iconic redstone engineers and one of its longest-serving members. He gave the welcome speech at the start of Season 10, introducing new members SmallishBeans and Skizzleman.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [MumboJumbo](https://www.youtube.com/user/ThatMumboJumbo)
- **Known for:** Redstone tutorials, Hermitcraft series, "This Minecraft Farm is TOO OP" style videos

## Hermitcraft History

Joined Season 2 on June 9, 2013 — his first video on the server was titled *"HermitCraft: Episode 0 - Welcome Home"*. Has been active across virtually every vanilla season since.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Mumbo is the server's premier **redstone engineer**. His contraptions are often so complex that other Hermits have said they are too complicated to understand. He specialises in:
- Large-scale automation systems
- Industrial farms
- Redstone "smart" bases with automated functions

He often self-deprecates about his building aesthetics, calling his builds "gray boxes", which became a long-running server joke.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 5:** Ran for Hermit Council Mayor, losing to Scar.
- **Season 7:** Built a highly automated base featuring a **self-building/self-repairing house**; deeply involved in the Mycelium Resistance.
- **Season 8:** Contributed builds to the unusual one-chunk-per-person Season 8 world.
- **Season 9:** Part of the **Boatem** group; built the iconic Boatem "hole" base.
- **Season 10:** Built an automated database at his survival base; built an automatic bamboo farm; featured "smart redstone contraptions" throughout. Delivered the Season 10 welcome speech.

## Teams & Groups

- **Architechs (Season 7):** MumboJumbo, Grian, Iskall85 — built the mega-shop **"Sahara"** (Hermitcraft's version of Amazon)
- **The Buttercups:** MumboJumbo, Grian, GoodTimesWithScar
- **Boatem (Season 9):** MumboJumbo, Grian, Scar, PearlescentMoon, Impulse

## Notable In-Jokes / Trivia

- His **moustache** is one of the most iconic features in the Hermitcraft community — he shaved it off in a memorable video moment.
- Frequently the subject of pranks, especially from Grian.
- His "gray box" building style became a long-running gag.
- Often says builds are "absolutely mental" or "insane" in his videos.
