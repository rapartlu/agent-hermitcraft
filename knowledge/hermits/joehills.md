# JoeHills

## Overview

JoeHills joined Hermitcraft in **Season 1** in May 2012, making him one of the **five original Hermits** (alongside Xisumavoid and a few others from the very first days). He is an American YouTuber known for his uniquely creative and literary video editing style, clever wordplay, and storytelling approach to Minecraft content — setting him apart from most other members.

## YouTube Channel

- **Channel:** [JoeHills](https://www.youtube.com/c/JoeHills)

## Hermitcraft History

One of the original Season 1 members. First appeared in **Season 1, Week 5**. Has remained active across all seasons.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Joe is in a category of his own — his specialty is:
- **Unique video editing** — his videos are edited with a distinct literary/artistic flair unlike any other Hermit
- **Storytelling** — frames his Minecraft content as narrative with character and voice
- **Wordplay and wit** — his content is full of clever language and literary references
- **Creative projects** that reflect his broader artistic interests

## Notable Builds & Projects

- Consistent base builds each season with a unique narrative framing.
- **Hedge Games:** Participated as part of team "CLeojoestressss" with ZombieCleo and StressMonster101.
- Active in server-wide events and storylines.
