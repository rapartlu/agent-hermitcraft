# SmallishBeans

## Overview

SmallishBeans (known as "Joel") officially joined Hermitcraft as a full member in **Season 10** (January 2024), alongside Skizzleman — both announced as the new members for that season. Joel had appeared on the server twice as a guest in Season 9 before being invited to join permanently. He is a British YouTuber known for his extravagant building style and entertaining personality.
