# Skizzleman

## Overview

Skizzleman (known as "Skizz") officially joined Hermitcraft as a full member in **Season 10** (January 2024), alongside SmallishBeans. Before joining, he participated in **Hermit Quest Season 1**. He is known for his genuine kindness, unique builds, and warm community presence — widely regarded as one of the most wholesome members of the server.
