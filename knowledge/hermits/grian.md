# Grian

## Overview

Grian (real name Charles Batchelor, born August 9, 1993) is a British Minecraft YouTuber and one of the most popular members of Hermitcraft. He joined in Season 6 (2018) and has been active ever since. As of early 2026, his main channel has approximately **8.88 million subscribers** and around 2.77 billion total views, making him the largest Hermitcraft creator by subscriber count.

## Specialty & Playstyle

Grian is primarily known as a **builder and prankster**. His building style — sometimes called "Grian Style" — emphasises variety, detail, and asymmetry, contrasting with Mojang's more uniform aesthetic. He is also renowned for elaborate pranks on fellow Hermits and for driving server-wide events and storylines.

Beyond Hermitcraft, Grian co-founded **Wynncraft**, the largest MMORPG built inside Minecraft. In 2021 he launched the **Life Series**, a collaborative limited-life Minecraft series featuring ~14 YouTubers.

## Notable Builds & Projects

- **Season 6:** Joined the server and quickly became embroiled in the "Barge" war with Mumbo Jumbo.
- **Season 7:** Built an elaborate base and was heavily involved in the **Mycelium Resistance** vs. HEP (Hermit Environmental Protection) storyline.
- **Season 8:** Built the **"Big Base"** on the one-chunk world border system unique to Season 8.
- **Season 9:** Participated in the server's "Boatem" group with Mumbo, Scar, PearlescentMoon, and others.
- **Season 10:** Active building and participation in server events.
